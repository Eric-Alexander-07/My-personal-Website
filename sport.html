Training pillars

I love combining structured training with adventure. Splitting the week between endurance, skill work, and strength keeps me on track and ready for the next race or performance.

Each discipline feeds the other: explosive starts from sprint sessions help with jumps, balance drills from dance improve calisthenics holds, and ice-cold open water swims train resilience for obstacle runs.
